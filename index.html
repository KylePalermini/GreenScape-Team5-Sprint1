About GreenScape

At GreenScape, we believe your lawn is more than just grass—it is the first impression of your home and a sanctuary for your family. Founded on the principles of precision and unwavering reliability, GreenScape was built to bridge a gap in the industry: the need for a professional partner who honors their schedule and treats every property with an owner’s eye for detail. With over 30 years of experience in the lawn care and landscaping industry, we have built a reputation for excellence backed by more than 100 five-star reviews. We specialize in comprehensive turf health—including aeration and seeding, landscape maintenance, and customized lawn treatments. When you choose GreenScape, you aren’t just hiring a service; you’re investing in thirty years of proven expertise.
